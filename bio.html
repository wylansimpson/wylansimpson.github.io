Wylan's Biography


Wylan was born in Pacifica, CA, but spent most of his childhood and high school years in Arcata, CA. A lifelong athlete, Wylan has played soccer, basketball, ran track and played rugby throughout his life. Also an avid outdoorsman, Wylan loves to return home to hike and mountain bike in the redwood forests and wild mountains of Northern California.

Academically, Wylan studies Economics and Computer Science at Tufts University, where he is in his senior year. Wylan is working on a Senior Honors Thesis which analyzes the characteristics of value-creating M&A transactions from an empirical perspective.
